# Style Vocabulary

Use this to turn vague taste words into visible instructions. Choose a few precise terms, not a shopping list.

## Camera And Lens

| Need | Useful language |
|---|---|
| Product hero | `three-quarter front angle`, `centered packshot`, `eye-level product view`, `100mm macro detail` |
| Portrait/key art | `35mm cinematic portrait`, `85mm shallow depth of field`, `low-angle heroic framing` |
| Interior/lifestyle | `24mm environmental wide shot`, `50mm natural perspective`, `over-the-shoulder view` |
| Texture/macro | `100mm macro`, `razor-thin focus plane`, `specular highlights`, `visible surface texture` |
| Flat layout | `top-down studio view`, `orthographic layout`, `grid-aligned composition` |

## Lighting

| Mood | Useful language |
|---|---|
| Premium clean | `large softbox key light`, `controlled rim light`, `soft shadow falloff`, `neutral white balance` |
| Skincare fresh | `natural morning side light`, `water-reflected highlights`, `translucent glow`, `soft diffused daylight` |
| Luxury dramatic | `warm golden key light`, `deep controlled shadows`, `thin edge highlights`, `polished reflective accents` |
| Cinematic | `volumetric backlight`, `selective highlights`, `low-key contrast`, `atmospheric haze` |
| Neon/night | `cyan-magenta neon spill`, `wet reflective surfaces`, `hard rim light`, `deep black negative space` |

## Composition

- `clear focal hierarchy`
- `strong readable silhouette`
- `centered hero composition`
- `diagonal action line`
- `layered foreground / midground / background`
- `negative space reserved for title`
- `title-safe area at the top`
- `rule-of-thirds product placement`
- `symmetrical editorial layout`
- `modular ecommerce sections`

## Materials And Texture

- Glass: `realistic glass thickness`, `edge refraction`, `subtle caustics`, `controlled reflections`
- Plastic: `matte molded plastic`, `soft edge highlights`, `not overly glossy`
- Metal: `brushed metal`, `anodized finish`, `crisp specular line`
- Skincare: `translucent gel`, `silky foam`, `dewy droplets`, `creamy texture`, `frosted pump`
- Paper/packaging: `crisp printed label`, `embossed paper texture`, `sharp ink edges`
- Food/product render: `fresh surface moisture`, `natural imperfections`, `non-plastic texture`

## Palette

- Clean beauty: `soft white`, `mist blue`, `translucent pale green`, `warm stone`, `cream`
- Luxury: `champagne gold`, `deep navy`, `charcoal black`, `pearl`, `warm ivory`
- Clinical tech: `cool white`, `glass blue`, `silver`, `cyan accent`, `neutral gray`
- Cinematic danger: `toxic orange`, `cobalt blue`, `smoke gray`, `black negative space`
- Soft illustration: `muted watercolor palette`, `warm natural greens`, `paper cream`, `dusty rose`

## Rendering Medium

- Photorealistic commercial photography
- Editorial product photography
- Premium CGI product render
- Tilt-shift miniature diorama
- Hand-drawn watercolor with ink line work
- Flat vector illustration with clean geometric shapes
- Painterly cinematic key art
- Polished 3D animated feature render
- Technical exploded-view visualization

## Negative Constraints By Failure

| Failure risk | Constraint |
|---|---|
| Random text | `No extra text, no fake labels, no watermark` |
| Product drift | `Do not redraw, relabel, resize, recolor, or rebrand the product` |
| CGI plastic look | `Avoid over-polished CGI shine, plastic-looking surfaces, and impossible reflections` |
| Poster clutter | `No crowded background, no small unreadable symbols, no random logos` |
| Character drift | `No outfit drift, no face drift, no inconsistent proportions` |
| Typography failure | `Keep text short, high contrast, perfectly legible, and placed once` |

## Taste Translations

- `高级`: restrained composition, expensive materials, controlled light, fewer props, clear negative space.
- `氛围感`: atmosphere, light direction, shadow depth, color temperature, environmental texture.
- `大片感`: strong silhouette, cinematic lens, dramatic lighting, scale contrast, title-safe composition.
- `干净`: minimal props, high-key lighting, soft shadows, neutral palette, crisp edges.
- `科技感`: glass, metal, cool white/cyan palette, modular layout, precise geometry.
- `治愈`: warm natural light, soft edges, gentle palette, tactile material, calm spacing.
